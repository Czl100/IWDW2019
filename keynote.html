Keynote speakers

Qiang Yang, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, Hongkong

Qiang Yang is New Bright Professor of Engineering, Chair Professor and Head of Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. His research interests include Data Ming and Artificial Intelligence: Transfer Learning, Lifelong Machine Learning and Planning. He was selected as an IEEE Fellow for Significant Contributions to the Understanding and Application of Intelligent Planning, Learning and Data Mining in 2009.He was selected as an IAPR Fellow for significant contributions to Data Mining and Transfer Learning in 2012. He was selected as an AAAS Fellow for significant contributions to Data Mining, Learning and Planning: theory, applications, and services in 2012.He was selected as an AAAI Fellow for significant contributions to AI Planning, Data Mining and Case-based Reasoning in 2013.

Hui Lei, IBM USA

Hui Lei is Chief Technology Officer, Watson Health Cloud, at IBM, and is a member of the IBM Academy of Technology. He provides leadership on the Watson Health Cloud technical strategy, and spearheads the development of the Watson Health Cloud. The IBM strategic Watson Health Cloud brings together clinical, research and social data from diverse sources, supports advanced cognitive and analytic capabilities, empowers a wide spectrum of health solutions for enhancing and scaling human experiences, and delivers an open, secure and massively scalable innovation platform for ecosystem partners and clients.
